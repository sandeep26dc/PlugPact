## 🛠️ ENGINEERING STACK
Designed by **Sandeep Som** for maximum efficiency and stability:

*   **UI Engine:** Jetpack Compose (Modern Declarative Design).
*   **Graphics:** AGSL (Android Graphics Shading Language) for GPU-accelerated visuals.
*   **Background Core:** Lifecycle-aware Foreground Services for 24/7 autonomous monitoring.
*   **State Matrix:** Kotlin Coroutines & StateFlow for lag-free hardware updates.

---

## 🔒 PRIVACY MANIFESTO
*   **Zero-Internet Policy:** No data transmission capabilities exist in the source code.
*   **No Trackers:** Strictly no analytics, ads, or third-party SDKs.
*   **Hardware Safety:** Engineered to reduce thermal stress and chemical wear.
